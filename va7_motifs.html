Valsgärde 7

Motifs of Vendel Period Helmet Plates and Related Objects

The enumaration of motifs follows an upcoming MA Thesis and includes more than only the motifs of the Va. 7 helmet.

M1: Man between the Bears

Reconstruction of the motif on Va7 by Arwidsson (1977)

This motif is present on the Va. 7 helmet as two fragments of the fourth plate row positioned frontal on the left and right side.

North-Germanic context this motif is known from the Torslunda Patrice A helmet.

Similar motifs are found on S

M2: Binding of the Beast

Torslunda B

This motif is not present on the Va. 7 helmet.

M3: Warrior Procession

M3-1: Warrior Procession with Boar-Helmets

Reconstruction of the motif on Va7 by Arwidsson (1977)

This motif is present on the first plate row on the left part of the Va. 7 helmet.

M3-2: Warrior Procession with Eagle-Helmets

Reconstruction of the motif on Va7 by Arwidsson (1977)

This motif is present on the first plate row on the right part of the Va. 7 helmet.

M4: The Duelers

Ve12

This motif is present on the second plate row on the left part of the Va. 7 helmet.

M5: The Sword Dancers (Waffentänzer)

M5-1: Single Sword Dancer

Patrice from Vejleby, DK. Søren Greve, National Museum of Denmark (CC-BY-SA)

This motif is present on the third plate row on the left part of the Va. 7 helmet.

M5-2: Pair of Sword Dancers

Reconstrution of the motif on Va7 by Arwidsson (1977)

This motif is present on the third plate row on the right part of the Va. 7 helmet.

M-6 (or 5-3): "Horned" Figure with an Anthropomorphic Wolf-Like

Torslunda D

In North-Germanic context it is only present on Torslunda Patrice D.

Two Continental Germanic finds depicting a similar wolf-like figures are known: the scabbard of Gutenstein and a fragment of Obrigheim.

M7: The Horserider Motif

M7-1: Horserider with Animal Companions

Reconstruction of the motif on Ve1 by Stolpe & Arne (1912)

This motif is present on the fourth plate row on the left part of the Va. 7 helmet.

M7-2: Horserider with Anthropomorphic Companion(s)

Reconstruction of the motif on Ve1 by Stolpe & Arne (1912)

This motif is present on the fourth plate row on the left part of the Va. 7 helmet.

M7-3: Horserider with Anthropomorphic Companion(s) and Enemies

Reconstruction of the motif on Va7 by Arwidsson (1977)

This motif is present on the fourth plate row on the left part of the Va. 7 helmet.

M8: Kneeling Warriors

Reconstruction of the motif on the Staffordshire Helmet

This motif is present on the fourth plate row on the left part of the Va. 7 helmet.
